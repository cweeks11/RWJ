About Us

Roadway Jungle Driving School is a premier driver education center located in Columbia, SC. We are committed to equipping students of all ages with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to become safe, responsible drivers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable driver education for all ages and skill levels, ensuring every student feels confident and prepared behind the wheel.

FAQ

What does SC require? All 15- & 16-year-olds must take a course before being eligible for a SC driver’s license, including 40 hours of behind-the-wheel training and 8 hours of classroom time.

Do I need a permit to take the course? A permit is required for behind-the-wheel training but not for the classroom portion.

Can I take the road test through Roadway Jungle? Yes, we offer a state-certified driver’s license test.
