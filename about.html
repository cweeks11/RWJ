About Us

Roadway Jungle Driving School is a premier driver education center located in Columbia, SC. We are committed to equipping students of all ages with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to become safe, responsible drivers.

FAQ’s

What does SC require? All 15- & 16-year-olds are required to take the course before being eligible for a SC driver’s license. Have a SC learner’s permit for 180 days. 40 hours of behind-the-wheel (BTW) training. 8 hours of classroom time.

Do I need a permit to take the course? You do not need a permit for the classroom portion but it is required for behind-the-wheel training.

Can I take the road test through Roadway Jungle? Yes, we offer a state-certified test for a driver’s license.
